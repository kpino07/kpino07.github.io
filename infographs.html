Nothing Hits Harder Than the Truth of Concussions

This infographic shows how Chronic Traumatic Encephalopathy (CTE) is impacting the NFL. CTE is a progressive, degenerative disease afflicting the brain of people, such as athletes who take part in contact sports, who have suffered repeated trauma to the brain. The information presents research which has found that of 128 football players whose brain tissue was examined posthumously, nearly 80% tested positive for CTE. The infographic also details some of the efforts that are being made by the League to increase player safety.

Could 22 Million Americans be in Jeopardy of Losing Health Care?

With Donald Trump elected to the White House and Republicans controlling Congress, more than 20 million Americans are in danger of losing health care coverage. This infographic illustrates public opinion on the issue of Obamacare, with a breakdown of those in favor of the law and those opposed, as well as state participation in programs such as Medicaid expansion.
